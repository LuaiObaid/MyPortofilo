About Me

Software Engineer & Social Enthusiast

I am a software engineer with a passion for social interactions, football, meeting new people, and traveling. I have diverse experience working in both large companies and startups, honing my skills in full-stack and embedded development.

At Knightec, I served as a full-stack/embedded developer, contributing to projects involving water level monitoring in plants, self-driving cars, and real-time temperature data analysis. I collaborated closely with cross-functional teams to ensure seamless integration of hardware and software components.

During my tenure at Hestia Agora, a startup based in Malmö, I worked as a full-stack and React Native developer. In this role, I contributed to the development of web, iOS, and Android applications aimed at assisting individuals working with elderly domestic services. These applications included robust security features, allowing users to log in using their bank IDs. Additionally, we implemented features enabling users to assess the situation of the elderly and send reports to the relevant municipalities. I gained valuable experience in managing large-scale database systems, including those serving over 12 municipalities.
